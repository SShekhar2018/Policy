Privacy Policy

Last updated: 01 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how HealthTrace ("the App") handles data.

1. Data Collection by the App

The App does not collect, transmit, store on servers, or share any personal data.

The App itself does not collect:

  • Names or contact details
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Advertising or tracking data

No data is sent from the App to us.

2. Health Data (Apple HealthKit / Google Health Connect)

The App may access health data from Apple HealthKit or Google Health Connect only after you explicitly grant permission.

Health data:

  • Is accessed only with your consent
  • Is used only within the App
  • Is stored only locally on your device
  • Is never uploaded to any server
  • Is never shared with third parties
  • Is never used for advertising or tracking

You can revoke permissions at any time through your device settings.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • All App data remains on your device only
  • The App does not use cloud storage or backend servers
  • Reasonable technical measures are used to protect locally stored data

However, the security of locally stored data also depends on your device’s security. We do not guarantee absolute security and are not responsible for unauthorized access resulting from device-level vulnerabilities.

4. Data Export

The App allows you to export your data in CSV format.

Once exported:

  • The data is outside the App's control
  • You are solely responsible for protecting exported data
  • We are not responsible for misuse, loss, or unauthorized access

5. Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time using in-app options:

  • Permanent deletion of all data
  • Deletion of older data using built-in cleanup options

Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data. Deleted data cannot be recovered.

6. Analytics & Platform Services

The App does not use analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Operating systems and app distribution platforms (such as iOS, Android, Apple App Store, or Google Play Store) may collect limited technical or diagnostic information according to their own privacy policies. We do not access or control this information.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
