Privacy Policy

Last updated: 09 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how VitalTrace ("the App") handles your data. By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

1. Data Controller

VitalTrace
Email: health.trace26@gmail.com

2. Core Privacy Principle

VitalTrace is designed with a privacy-first approach. All health data processing occurs locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, store on external servers, or otherwise access your personal health data.

3. Data Collection

The App itself does not collect or transmit any personal data, including:

  • Names or contact details
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Advertising or tracking data

No health or personal data is sent from the App to us.

If you contact us via email, we will process the information you provide solely to respond to your request.

4. Health Data (Apple HealthKit / Google Health Connect)

Important: VitalTrace will never use or share data from Apple HealthKit or Google Health Connect for advertising, marketing, or with data brokers. Health data is never transmitted to external servers.

The App may access health-related data only after you explicitly grant permission. Health data:

  • Is accessed only with your explicit consent
  • Is processed exclusively on your device
  • Is never transmitted to external servers
  • Is never shared with the developer or third parties
  • Is never used for advertising or tracking
  • Is never used with data brokers

We do not have access to your health data at any time.

The App provides informational insights only and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

You can revoke permissions at any time through your device settings (iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Health; Android: Settings > Apps > Health Connect).

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services which may process limited non-health data under their own privacy policies:

Google Sign-In

We offer sign-in via Google OAuth. When you use Google Sign-In, Google processes your authentication credentials under Google's Privacy Policy. We receive only your name and email address to create your account. This data is stored locally on your device.

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions and purchases. RevenueCat may process limited purchase-related data (such as your App Store or Google Play purchase receipt) to validate your subscription. RevenueCat does not receive any health data. For details, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Apple Inc. / Google LLC

Platform providers (Apple Inc. and Google LLC) may collect limited technical data under their own privacy policies when you use the App Store or Google Play. We do not access or control this data.

6. Data Storage & Security

All health and personal data remains stored locally on your device using encrypted local storage. We implement reasonable technical measures within the App; however, data security also depends on your device security settings. We cannot guarantee absolute security and are not responsible for unauthorized access caused by device-level vulnerabilities.

7. Data Export

The App may allow you to export your data (e.g., PDF or CSV format). Once exported:

  • The data is outside the App's control
  • You are solely responsible for protecting it
  • We are not responsible for misuse, loss, or unauthorized access

8. Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time within the App settings. Deleted data cannot be recovered. Deleting the App from your device will also remove all locally stored data.

9. Legal Basis (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and United Kingdom, processing is based on:

  • Your explicit consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR)
  • Explicit consent for special category health data (Article 9(2)(a) GDPR)

You may withdraw your consent at any time by revoking permissions in your device settings or deleting the App. All processing takes place locally on your device.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

VitalTrace does not sell personal information. All data remains on your device. To exercise your rights, contact us at health.trace26@gmail.com.

11. International Data Transfers

VitalTrace is available worldwide. Your health data is stored exclusively on your device and is not transferred internationally by us. Third-party services (Google Sign-In, RevenueCat) may transfer limited authentication or purchase data internationally under their own policies and applicable data transfer mechanisms.

12. Your Rights

You have the following rights related to your data:

  • Right of access

  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

Because we do not store your data externally, most data control can be exercised directly within the App or by contacting us at health.trace26@gmail.com.

Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical advice. Do not make medical decisions based solely on information provided by this App.
