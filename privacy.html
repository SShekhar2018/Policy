Privacy Policy

Last updated: 09 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how HealthTrace ("the App") handles your data

1. Data Controller

HealthTrace
Email: health.trace26@gmail.com

2. Core Privacy Principle

HealthTrace is designed with a privacy-first approach. All data processing occurs locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, store on external servers, or otherwise access your personal data.

3. Data Collection

The App itself does not collect or transmit any personal data, including:

  • Names or contact details
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Advertising or tracking data

No data is sent from the App to us.

If you contact us via email, we will process the information you provide solely to respond to your request.

4. Health Data (Apple HealthKit / Google Health Connect)

The App may access health-related data only after you explicitly grant permission.

Health data:

  • Is accessed only with your explicit consent
  • Is processed exclusively on your device
  • Is never transmitted to external servers
  • Is never shared with the developer or third parties
  • Is never used for advertising or tracking

We do not have access to your health data at any time.

The App provides informational insights only and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

You can revoke permissions at any time through your device settings.

5. Data Storage & Security

All data remains stored locally on your device. We implement reasonable technical measures within the App; however, data security also depends on your device. We cannot guarantee absolute security and are not responsible for unauthorized access caused by device-level vulnerabilities.

6. Data Export

The App may allow you to export your data (e.g., CSV format). Once exported:

  • The data is outside the App's control
  • You are solely responsible for protecting it
  • We are not responsible for misuse, loss, or unauthorized access

7. Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time within the App.

Deleted data cannot be recovered.

8. Analytics & Third-Party Services

The App does not use analytics tools, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, or external data processing services.

However, platform providers such as Apple Inc. and Google LLC may collect limited technical data under their own privacy policies. We do not access or control this data.

9. Legal Basis

Where processing occurs, it is based on:

  • Your consent
  • Explicit consent for health data

All processing takes place locally on your device.

10. Your Rights

You have the following rights related to your data:

  • Right of access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

Because we do not store your data externally, most data control can be exercised directly within the App.

11. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 18 years of age.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be published within the App or on the app store listing.
